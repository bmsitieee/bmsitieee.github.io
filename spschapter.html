IEEE SIGNAL PROCESSING SOCIETY

OUR MISSION

The Signal Processing Society is an international organization whose purpose is to: advance and disseminate state-of-the-art scientific information and resources, educate the signal processing community, and provide a venue for people to interact and exchange ideas.

OUR VISION

Be a one-stop source of signal processing resources; providing a variety of high quality resources to a variety of users in formats customized to their interests; adapting to a rapidly changing technical community; and being intimately involved in the education of signal processing professionals at all levels.

    WHY TO JOIN IEEE SPS CHAPTER??

  • Advance your knowledge and access cutting-edge resources in signal processing.
  • Connect with a diverse global community for learning and idea exchange.
  • Benefit from tailored workshops, webinars, and resources for all career levels.
  • Stay informed with the latest developments through expert-led events and publications.
  • Unlock exclusive opportunities, including scholarships, to boost your career.
